Manal Chaib

"I photograph the spaces between — the glance before the smile, the pause before the word, the quiet after the storm."

Manal Chaib is a photographer drawn to raw light and unguarded moments. Her work spans portraiture, documentary, street, and editorial photography — always in search of the authentic over the perfect.

Based between cities, she brings a traveler's eye to every shoot: attuned to light, shadow, and the small gestures that make a photograph feel true. Her images have been used for editorial publications, personal commissions, and exhibitions.

She shoots primarily on digital but frequently turns to film for its grain, warmth, and the forced deliberateness it demands.
